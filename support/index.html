Help & FAQs for WorldCup 2026

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I see today's matches?

Open the app and tap the "Today" tab (soccer ball icon). You'll see all matches happening today, including live scores and upcoming kickoffs.

How do I view the full match schedule?

Tap the "Schedule" tab (calendar icon) to browse all 104 matches grouped by date, from the group stage through the final.

Where can I find group standings?

Tap the "Standings" tab (numbered list icon) to see group tables with points, wins, draws, losses, goals for, goals against, and total points.

How do I remove ads?

Go to the "More" tab and tap "Remove All Ads". It's a one-time purchase that removes banner ads permanently across all screens.

How do match notifications work?

The app sends a notification 15 minutes before a match starts. You'll be prompted to grant notification permission when you first open the app.

How do I see the knockout bracket?

Tap the "More" tab and select "Knockout Bracket" to view the tournament bracket from the Round of 32 through the Final.

How do I save my favorite teams?

Go to the "More" tab, tap "Teams", then tap the star icon next to any team to add it to your favorites for quick access.

Why does the app use data?

The app fetches live match data from our API. Scores update automatically every 30 seconds when there are live matches.
