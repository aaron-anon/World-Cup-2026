Privacy Policy

WorldCup 2026 App

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Information We Collect

WorldCup 2026 does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device and does not transmit user data to any server.

Data Storage

The app uses local storage on your device for the following purposes:

  • Cache: Match data, team data, and stadium data are cached locally to reduce network usage and provide offline access.
  • Preferences: Your favorite teams, ad removal purchase status, and notification preferences are stored locally on your device.

All stored data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Network Requests

The app makes requests to our API server at worldcup26.ir (with a fallback to wc2026.moothz.win) to fetch match data, team information, and stadium details. No personal information is included in these requests.

Notifications

If you grant permission, the app sends local notifications to alert you 15 minutes before a match starts. No push notifications are used, and no data is sent to any server for notification purposes.

Advertising

The app displays banner ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can remove all ads with a one-time in-app purchase. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more information on their data collection practices.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through Apple's StoreKit. We do not receive or store any payment or financial information. Purchase records are stored locally to manage ad removal status.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.
